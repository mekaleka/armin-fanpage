Biography

Armin Van Buuren is a trance music icon. He created "A State Of Trance" in 2001 and has been one of the most influential artists in trance music around the world since. According to Wikipedia his radio show and podcast (A State Of Trance) has reached over 40 million listeners in 84 countries. He has been on over 40 radio stations and has been ranked as the number one DJ in the world by DJ Mag five times. In 2014, he was nominated for a Grammy for his single "This Is What It Feels Like" featuring Trevor Guthrie, a Canadian singer and songwriter. In the US, Armin holds the record for the most entries on the Billboard Dance/Electronic Albums chart, for a total of twenty-one.

Armin was born in Leiden Holland on December 25th 1976. He started making music at age 14 and graduated from Stedelijk Gymnasium Leiden in 1995. His career later took off in 1999 when he was announced as a DJ in England and in the US by Dave Lewis. Armin is married to his wife Erica and they have two children, a girl named Fenna and a boy Remy. Armin currently has 7 studio albums and is still producing music today. He has been appointed as an Officer of the "Order of Orange Nassau" (a Dutch order of chivalry) for his services to music along with an ongoing list of awards and nominations.
